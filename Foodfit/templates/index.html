About Nutrition Analysis

" Your one-stop solution for tracking and analyzing your daily food intake.

Whether you want to maintain a healthy diet or have specific dietary goals

in mind, our platform can help you keep a record of what you eat and provide

valuable insights into your nutrition. "

Key Features

  • User Registration: Create an account to get started.
  • User Login: Log in to access your personalized food intake data.
  • Admin Login: Administrators can manage food data.
  • Food Intake Logging: Record your daily meals and snacks.
  • Nutritional Information Search: Look up nutritional facts for specific foods.
